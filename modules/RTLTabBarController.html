Module RTLTabBarController

Smartface RTLTabBarController To Support RIGHT-TO-LEFT Languages RTLTabBarController class is inherited from TabBarController. It manipulates the index/array based functions/properties to support RTL languages. Such as, in case of app direction is RTL, array & index values are reversed.

Note: Returned indexes or arrays won't impact the logic.
